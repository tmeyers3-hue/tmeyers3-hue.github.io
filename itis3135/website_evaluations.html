Website Evaluations

Good Crap Site

This site serves as a landing page for an interior design studio

  • Contrast

    This site uses contrast to guide the viewer through the webpage. For example on the first view of the page, the two main elements by size are their logo and an example of their work. The remaining crucial elements are much smaller and unobtrusive.

  • Repetition

    The site uses a consistent grid approach for structuring its content

  • Alignment

    The aformentioned grid aligns the site into four quartiles vertically. This allows the content to be sectioned off in various ways while keeping contents aligned.

  • Proximity

    The grid allows the content to be spaced evenly and not get cluttered.

Bad Crap Site

This site is the storefront for a discount bed seller in the UK

  • Contrast

    Most of the body of the website is the same default white, not providing much color contrast. Bold and Italics are used inconsistently.

  • Repetition

    Details under some listings for beds are left out in others. The page never establishes sections that could be repeated, and opts for a long string of differing information.

  • Alignment

    The page is mostly one running column which doesn't make for many alignment issues, but certain elements appear in different places as you scroll.

  • Proximity

    Plenty of examples of large blocks of white space or random empty lines
